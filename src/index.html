Frequently Asked Questions

What is CryptoMarketer AI?

CryptoMarketer AI is an all-in-one marketing automation platform specifically designed for crypto projects. It combines AI-powered content creation, social media management, community engagement, airdrop distribution, referral tracking, and analytics in one seamless platform.

Why do I need to pay with Purple Bitcoin (PBTC)?

CryptoMarketer AI is exclusively powered by Purple Bitcoin to create a sustainable ecosystem. This model benefits both platform users and token holders by creating utility for PBTC, implementing token burns that reduce supply, and offering special perks to PBTC holders.

How do I get Purple Bitcoin (PBTC)?

You can acquire PBTC through several methods: 1) Use the "Swap for PBTC" button on our website to connect to a DEX, 2) Purchase directly on exchanges where PBTC is listed, or 3) Use the bridge function if you hold other cryptocurrencies. We recommend using the swap function for the most seamless experience.

Is my project data secure?

Absolutely. We implement industry-leading security practices including OAuth2 authentication, read-only API access until explicitly approved, and no storage of your social media passwords. All smart contracts are audited by top security firms, and we're fully compliant with data protection regulations.

How much time will this save my team?

Based on our pilot projects, teams save an average of 120+ hours per month on marketing tasks. This includes time previously spent on content creation, social media management, community moderation, and analytics reporting. Most users can set up their entire marketing stack in less than 20 minutes.

Can I customize the AI to match my project's voice?

Yes! During onboarding, you'll provide examples of your project's communication style, key terminology, and brand guidelines. Our AI adapts to your unique voice and continuously improves as you approve or edit content. You can also create custom templates for different types of communications.

What social platforms are supported?

We currently support Twitter, Discord, Telegram, LinkedIn, and Medium. Additional platforms including Instagram, Reddit, and TikTok are on our roadmap for Q3 2025. We prioritize platforms based on their relevance to crypto marketing and user requests.

How does the early founder incentive work?

The first 100 projects to join CryptoMarketer AI will lock in a lifetime rate of just 0.5 PBTC per month. This rate will never increase for these founding members, even as we add new features and raise prices for later subscribers. This is our way of rewarding early adopters who believe in our vision.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll maintain access until the end of your current billing period. Early founders who cancel will lose their special lifetime rate if they choose to resubscribe later.
